### Service Level Agreement (SLA)

**Uptime Guarantee:**
99.5% uptime guarantee for production platform (excludes scheduled maintenance).

**Scheduled Maintenance:**
Announced 48 hours in advance, limited to 4 hours per month, conducted during low-traffic periods (typically 1-4 AM IST).

**Unscheduled Downtime:**
- Critical issues: Response within 2 hours (Premium Support) or 4 hours (Standard Support)
- Major issues: Response within 4 hours (Premium) or 8 hours (Standard)
- Minor issues: Response within 24 business hours

**SLA Credits:**
If uptime falls below 99.5% in any month, client receives pro-rated credit on next month's support fee:
- 99.0% - 99.49% uptime: 10% credit
- 98.0% - 98.99% uptime: 25% credit
- Below 98.0% uptime: 50% credit

**Exclusions:**
Downtime caused by third-party services (payment gateways, DNS providers), DDoS attacks, or client-initiated changes not covered under SLA.
